Creamy Hummus


To make hummus, you will need the following items:

  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic
  • Parsley and Paprika (for garnish)

And you will also need a blender to mix up your ingredients.

Instructions

1.) Add 1 can of chickpeas to your blender

Your chickpeas may be precooked or you may cook your chickpeas yourself. For smoother hummus, you may remove the skin from each chickpea, although this is time-consuming.

2.) Add Olive Oil

Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to your blender. You may add an extra tablespoon for even smoother consistency.

3.) Add Lemon Juice

Add 2 tablespoons of lemon juice to your blender to give your hummus more flavor.

4.) Add Garlic

Add 2 cloves of minced garlic to your blender.

5.) Add Salt

Add no more than a pinch of salt to your ingredients.

6.) Blend!

Blend your ingredients until smooth!

7.) Add Parsley and Paprika

Add chopped parsley and paprika to garnish your hummus. You can also add extra olive oil, tomatoes, or basil on top. Serve with your favorite vegetable or pita bread for dipping.

Enjoy!
