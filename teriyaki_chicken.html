Teriyaki Chicken


To make teriyaki chicken, you will need the following items:

  • Chicken
  • Teriyaki Sauce and Sesame Oil
  • Rice Vinegar or Wine
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Soy or Tamari Sauce
  • Green Onions or Leeks

And you will also need a large pan to stir fry your chicken.

Instructions

1.) Make Marinade

Mix in 1/2 cup of teriyaki sauce, 1/2 cup of rice vinegar or wine, 1 cup of soy or tamari sauce, 3 cloves of minced garlic and 3 inches of minced and skinned ginger to a bowl. Mix ingredients well. Use rice wine and tamari sauce if available, as they will give your chicken a smoother taste.

2.) Add Chicken to your Marinade

Add 3-4 lbs of chopped boneless chicken breast to your marinade. Mix well, and let marinate for 30 minutes, longer if possible.

3.) Prepare your Pan

Lightly coat your pan with sesame oil and put it on heat.

4.) Add Chicken to your Pan

Add your chicken to your pan, and pour your remaining marinade into the pan as well.

5.) Stir Fry the Chicken

Using a spatula, stir fry your chicken until well done.

6.) Add Green Onions or Leeks for garnish

Add minced green onions or leeks to garnish your chicken. Serve with saut√©ed broccoli or other vegetables on a bed of steamed rice.

Enjoy!
