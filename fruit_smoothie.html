Fruit Smoothie


To make a smoothie, you will need the following items:

  • Fruit (preferably frozen)
  • Milk (can be dairy or non-dairy)
  • Yogurt (high-protein)
  • Flaxseed
  • Vanilla extract
  • Extra ingredients
  • Toppings (optional)

And you will also need a blender to mix up your ingredients.

Instructions

1.) Choose your fruit

Pick your favorite fruit and add 1 cup to your blender. Some common options include berries, bananas, or mangoes. For a thicker, smoothie-like consistency, you should preferably use frozen fruit. You can also add apple sauce or juice to add sweetness to your smoothie.

2.) Add Milk

Add 1 cup of milk to your blender. You may use dairy milk or non-dairy milk such as almond milk if you would like.

3.) Add Yogurt

Add 1/2 cup of high-protein yogurt to your blender. You may use dairy yogurt or non-dairy yogurt if you would like.

4.) Add Flaxseed and Vanilla Extract

Add 1 tablespoon of flaxseed and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the blender. This will make your smoothie thicker and tastier, not to mention the health benefits of flaxseed.

5.) Add more Ingredients

Add some of your favorite cooking ingredients as desired for more flavor. Some common choices include cocoa powder, peanut butter, cinnamon, or mint.

6.) Blend!

Blend your smoothie until smooth!

7.) Add toppings (optional)

If you would like, you may add toppings to your smoothie. Some good options are fruit, nuts, or mint as garnish.

Enjoy!
